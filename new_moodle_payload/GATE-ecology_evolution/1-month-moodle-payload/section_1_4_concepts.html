  4. Topic: Community Ecology Lesson: Species richness Clarification: Number of species present in a defined area/sample; a count metric sensitive to sampling effort. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  5. Topic: Community Ecology Lesson: Evenness Clarification: How similar species' abundances are within a community; high evenness means less dominance by few species. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  6. Topic: Community Ecology Lesson: Diversity indices Clarification: Quantitative measures combining richness and relative abundance (e.g., Shannon and Simpson indices) to compare communities. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  7. Topic: Community Ecology Lesson: Species-area relationship (SAR) Clarification: Empirical relationship where species richness increases with area, commonly modeled as \(S = cA^z\), used for scaling and extinction projections. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  8. Topic: Community Ecology Lesson: Theory of island biogeography Clarification: MacArthur-Wilson framework where island species richness reflects a dynamic equilibrium between colonization and extinction, shaped by island isolation and area.
