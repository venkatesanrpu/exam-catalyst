  6. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Contest competition Clarification: Interference competition; individuals can exclude/monopolize resources, producing inequality and favoring dominance. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  7. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Dominance relationships Clarification: Consistent asymmetries in aggressive interactions producing rank and hierarchies that structure access to resources and mating. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  8. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Kin selection Clarification: Selection acting through indirect fitness gains when helping relatives; formalized by Hamilton’s rule. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  9. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Altruism Clarification: Behaviors that reduce actor’s direct fitness while increasing recipient’s fitness; can evolve via relatedness, reciprocity, or group-structured mechanisms. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  10. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Reciprocity Clarification: Helping based on contingent return benefits over repeated interactions; requires recognition, memory, and low cheating. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  11. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Eusociality Clarification: Extreme sociality with reproductive division of labor, cooperative brood care, and overlapping generations; explained via kin selection and ecological factors. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced

Related Concepts

  1. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Hamilton’s rule Clarification: Altruism can evolve when \( rB > C \), where \(r\) is relatedness, \(B\) benefit to recipient, \(C\) cost to actor. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  2. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Inclusive fitness Clarification: Total genetic contribution via direct reproduction plus effects on relatives’ reproduction weighted by relatedness. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  3. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Direct vs indirect fitness Clarification: Direct fitness from own offspring; indirect fitness via aiding relatives; their sum underpins kin selection logic. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  4. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Mutualism Clarification: Cooperation benefiting both parties immediately; distinct from altruism and often easier to maintain. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  5. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Reciprocal altruism (Trivers) Clarification: Cooperation stabilized by repeated interactions, partner choice, and punishment/retaliation against defectors. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  6. Topic: Social Living Lesson: By-product benefits Clarification: Helping arises as a side-effect of selfish action; can mimic altruism without fitness costs. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  7. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Cheater problem Clarification: Risk that individuals take benefits without paying costs; central to evolution of cooperation. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  8. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Kin recognition and discrimination Clarification: Mechanisms to direct help toward relatives (familiarity, phenotype matching, spatial cues). Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  9. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Policing and punishment Clarification: Mechanisms that suppress cheating and stabilize cooperation (worker policing in insects, social punishment in humans). Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  10. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Game theory in social behavior Clarification: Strategic interaction models (Prisoner’s Dilemma, Hawk–Dove, Public Goods) explaining conflict and cooperation. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  11. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Group selection and multilevel selection Clarification: Selection acting among groups can favor cooperation when between-group variation persists and within-group cheating is controlled. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  12. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Life-history and ecological constraints on eusociality Clarification: Monogamy, fortress defense, progressive provisioning, and high benefits of staying can promote eusociality. Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced


  13. Topic: Social Living Lesson: Gene–culture coevolution Clarification: Human-specific feedback between cultural practices and selection pressures (norms, institutions, mating rules). Show: Study Notes | MCQ Basic | MCQ Intermediate | MCQ Advanced
