### Reorganized Units

**Unit 8.1: Semiconductor Fundamentals**

- Semiconductors in Equilibrium: Electron and hole statistics in intrinsic and extrinsic semiconductors

**Unit 8.2: Metal-Semiconductor Junctions**

- Metal-semiconductor junctions; Ohmic and rectifying contacts

**Unit 8.3: Semiconductor Devices**

- PN diodes, bipolar junction transistors, field effect transistors

**Unit 8.4: Analog Circuits**

- Negative and positive feedback circuits; oscillators, operational amplifiers, active filters

**Unit 8.5: Digital Electronics**

- Basics of digital logic circuits, combinational and sequential circuits, flip-flops, timers, counters, registers, A/D and D/A conversion


### Logic Behind the Split

**Unit 8.1** establishes semiconductor physics fundamentals, covering carrier statistics and doping as the foundation for all electronic devices.

**Unit 8.2** introduces metal-semiconductor interfaces and contact types, bridging basic semiconductor physics and device structures.

**Unit 8.3** covers fundamental semiconductor devices (diodes, BJTs, FETs) that serve as building blocks for all electronic circuits.

**Unit 8.4** groups analog circuit applications utilizing semiconductor devices, including feedback theory and signal processing circuits.

**Unit 8.5** consolidates all digital electronics topics from basic logic gates through complex systems (counters, registers) and conversion circuits, representing a complete digital electronics domain distinct from analog circuits.
